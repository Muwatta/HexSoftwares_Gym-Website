Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a joining fee?

No! We're currently waiving all joining fees. Sign up today and start immediately.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, Premium and Elite members can freeze their membership for up to 3 months per year.

What's the cancellation policy?

Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime. Yearly plans can be cancelled with 30 days notice after the first 3 months.
