Our Facilities

World-class equipment and amenities

Strength Training Zone

Our 8,000 sq ft strength area features over 200 pieces of premium equipment including free weights, plate-loaded machines, cable stations, and functional training areas.

  • 50+ free weight stations
  • 100+ strength machines
  • Dedicated deadlift platforms
  • Functional rig with monkey bars

Cardio Theater

50+ state-of-the-art cardio machines with personal entertainment screens, heart rate monitoring, and virtual training programs.

  • Treadmills with incline/decline
  • Ellipticals & cross-trainers
  • Stair climbers & rowers
  • Personal TV screens

Group Fitness Studios

Three dedicated studios equipped with premium sound systems, specialized flooring, and atmospheric lighting for the ultimate group workout experience.

  • Yoga/Pilates studio (1,500 sq ft)
  • Spin studio with 40 bikes
  • HIIT studio with rig system
  • Premium sound & lighting

Indoor Aquatic Center

Olympic-sized heated pool for lap swimming, aqua aerobics, and relaxation. Complete with hot tub and steam room.

  • 25-meter heated pool
  • 6 dedicated lap lanes
  • Hot tub & steam room
  • Aqua aerobics classes

Recovery & Wellness Spa

Complete recovery center with sauna, steam room, massage therapy, and cryotherapy for optimal post-workout recovery.

  • Dry sauna & steam room
  • Professional massage therapy
  • Cryotherapy chamber
  • NormaTec compression

Premium Amenities

Thoughtful touches that make your gym experience exceptional from start to finish.

  • Digital lockers with phone charging
  • Towel service & laundry
  • Protein bar & smoothie station
  • Free parking & WiFi
